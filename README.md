# 🧠 About the Project

The project transforms raw relational data into meaningful business insights that help stakeholders understand key performance metrics and identify growth opportunities. The analysis covers multiple business areas, including sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, order trends, revenue analysis, and shipping operations. 

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Clean and prepare the retail sales dataset by identifying and removing missing values.
- Explore the dataset to understand customer, product, and sales information.
- Analyze overall sales performance and transaction activity.
- Evaluate sales performance across different product categories.
- Identify top-performing customers based on total sales.
- Analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
- Discover monthly sales trends and identify the best-selling month each year.
- Examine transaction distribution by gender and product category.
- Classify sales into different time shifts (Morning, Afternoon, and Evening) to understand purchasing patterns.
- Generate business insights using SQL queries to support data-driven decision-making.
